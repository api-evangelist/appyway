AppyWay's Mapper tool now allows users to generate text-based traffic orders directly within the platform, eliminating the need to switch between systems for workflows that still require structured written documentation alongside digital maps. While digital maps form the heart of modern traffic management, certain statutory requirements mean the written word still carries legal weight. Users can manage, edit, and generate text-based orders entirely within Mapper or the Traffic Suite for a more streamlined workflow.
